Custom Brokerage Good People, Good Services

At MarchLog Limited, our expertise lies in providing unparalleled custom brokerage services, ensuring a smooth and compliant passage for your goods through the intricate web of international trade regulations. We understand the complexities of customs procedures and strive to simplify the process for our clients. Our dedicated team of experienced customs brokers is well-versed in the latest customs regulations, tariffs, and trade agreements. We act as your trusted partner in ensuring all necessary documentation is accurately prepared and submitted, facilitating the efficient clearance of your shipments.

Demonstrating a solid understanding and adeptness in Aircraft On Ground (AOG) situations, encompassing the swift and efficient coordination of resources to address and resolve critical aircraft maintenance issues.

Proficient in the handling of Over Hand Cargo:
Our team possesses the necessary expertise and skills to effectively manage and transport various types of cargo that require specialized care and attention. With a thorough understanding of the unique challenges associated with overhand cargo, we ensure meticulous planning and execution to guarantee the safe and efficient delivery of goods. Our experience in this field enables us to navigate complexities, optimize processes, and deliver exceptional service tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients.

Bulk Cargo Services:
Our expertise in bulk cargo services involves managing the logistics, handling, and transportation of large quantities of loose goods efficiently and cost-effectively. Our team is well-versed in the specific requirements and regulations associated with handling different types of bulk cargo, ensuring smooth operations from origin to destination. We ensure proper loading, stowing, and discharge of bulk cargo to minimize handling costs and maximize transportation efficiency.

Container Services:
At MarchLog Limited we specialize in managing containerized cargo, including both Full Container Load (FCL) and Less than Container Load (LCL) shipments, our services encompass container booking, loading, tracking, customs clearance, and delivery, providing end-to-end solutions for our clients' containerized cargo needs. With our expertise in container services, we optimize container utilization, minimize transit times, and ensure the safe and secure transportation of goods in compliance with international standards.

Bagged Cargo Services:
Our proficiency in bagged cargo services includes handling various types of bagged commodities, such as agricultural products, fertilizers, chemicals, and construction materials. We offer specialized solutions with best practices in handling, storage, and transportation of bagged cargo and ensuring proper stacking, securing, and ventilation, to prevent damage or contamination and to maintain product quality and integrity throughout the supply chain.

Oil & Gas Services:
We have extensive experience in providing specialized services for the oil & gas industry, including the transportation of crude oil, refined products, natural gas, and offshore equipment. Our services cover the entire oil & gas supply chain, from upstream exploration and production to downstream distribution and storage. With a focus on safety, compliance, and efficiency, we ensure the reliable and timely delivery of oil & gas cargo while adhering to industry regulations and standards.

Broker:
As a logistics broker, we facilitate the movement of goods by connecting shippers with carriers. We negotiate rates, handle documentation, and ensure smooth transactions, acting as intermediaries to streamline the shipping process for our clients.

General Cargo:
Our logistics services cover the transportation of general cargo, which includes a wide range of non-specialized goods such as consumer products, machinery, and equipment. We provide efficient handling and transportation solutions tailored to the specific requirements of each cargo type.

Perishable:
With expertise in handling perishable goods, we ensure the timely and safe transportation of temperature-sensitive items such as food, pharmaceuticals, and floral products. Our specialized handling procedures and temperature-controlled facilities guarantee the integrity of perishable cargo throughout the supply chain.

Medicament:
We specialize in the transportation of medicaments and pharmaceutical products, adhering to strict regulations and quality standards to ensure the safe and secure delivery of these sensitive goods. Our logistics services include specialized handling, temperature monitoring, and regulatory compliance to safeguard the integrity and efficacy of medicament shipments.

Products and Services Representatives:

With a focus on understanding our clients' objectives and market dynamics, we implement tailored strategies to effectively showcase their products and services, driving growth and fostering mutually beneficial partnerships.

Custom Brokerage FAQ Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why are the shipping rates so volatile?
    The shipping rate is inherently volatile due to its sensitivity to various external factors, such as Currency Fluctuations, Regulatory Changes, Market Demand, Aviation fuel and the likes. Shipping companies and customers alike must closely monitor market conditions and adapt their strategies to navigate the challenges posed by fluctuating shipping rates.
  • What are the usual methods of freight payment?
    freight payments can be made on prepaid terms, (payment at origin by mobile transfers, drafts, checks etc) or on charges collect terms (payment at destination on arrival of shipment).
  • Do you handle international household moves?
    Yes, we handle international household moves.
  • How and where can we pick up the cargo at destination?
    Cargo can either be cleared and picked up at the port of destination or cargo can be delivered to doorstep of preferred delivery address depending on the recipient’s choice.
  • What do I do if I have a complaint or issue with my shipment?
    If you encounter any issues with your shipment, please contact our customer service team immediately. We are committed to resolving any concerns promptly and ensuring your satisfaction.
  • How does MarchLog ensure sustainability in its operations?
    MarchLog is committed to sustainability and implements eco-friendly practices such as optimizing routes to reduce fuel consumption, using energy-efficient vehicles, and minimizing waste in our operations. We continuously strive to minimize our environmental footprint.
  • How can I track my shipment?
    You can track your shipment easily through our online tracking system. Simply enter your tracking number on our website or contact our customer service team for assistance.
  • What types of transportation options does MarchLog offer?
    MarchLog Logistics offers a variety of transportation options including ground freight, air freight, ocean freight, and intermodal transportation to accommodate different shipping needs.
